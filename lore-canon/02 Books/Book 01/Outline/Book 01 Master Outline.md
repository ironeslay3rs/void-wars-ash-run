# Book 01 Master Outline

## Role of Book 1
Book 1 is the grounded entry point into the wider saga.

## Important Tone Rule
Reality remains grounded through Book 1.
The world should still feel modern, tired, human, and believable.

## Track Here
- opening condition of Iron's life
- exhaustion and realism
- first fractures in reality
- first symbols / first impossible signs
- emotional hook
- ending state that opens the greater saga

## Do Not Lose
- humanity
- emotional realism
- struggle
- subtle dread before open transformation

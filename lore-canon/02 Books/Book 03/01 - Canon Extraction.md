# Book 03 — Canon Extraction

## World Rules Introduced
- The world begins reacting to individuals who change.
- Systems of control, observation, and pressure emerge.
- Stability is not guaranteed; reality can destabilize under stress.

## Character Foundations
- Iron is no longer invisible.
- His presence begins to influence external systems.
- He is now something that can be tracked, studied, or targeted.

## System Hints
- There are forces that monitor change.
- The world itself may be structured as a system.
- Power attracts attention.

## Tone Rules
- instability
- surveillance
- pressure
- rising tension
- loss of safety

## Important Scenes
- first signs of being noticed
- external system reaction
- escalation beyond personal survival

## Notes To Move Into Canon
- The world is not passive.
- Change creates consequence.
- Systems exist to detect and respond to anomalies.

# Adaptation Law

The Evolution: Void Wars IP exists across multiple media.

## Core Rule
All media belong to the same universe,
but each medium may present events differently for pacing, structure, and audience fit.

## What Must Stay Stable
- core canon truths
- character identity
- world laws
- major themes
- evolution logic
- school logic
- the deeper humanity theme

## What Can Change By Medium
- scene order
- compression
- dialogue length
- visual emphasis
- pacing
- structure of reveals

## Important Rule
Adaptations may translate.
They may simplify.
They may rearrange.
They must not betray the core truth of the IP.

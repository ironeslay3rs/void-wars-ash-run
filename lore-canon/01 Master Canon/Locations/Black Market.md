# Black Market

The Black Market is one of the most important locations in the IP.

## Core Idea
It is not the strongest place.
It is the most interesting place.

## Role
The Black Market acts as a neutral or semi-neutral meeting ground,
a survivor city,
and a place where all forms of trade, power, corruption, and adaptation intersect.

## Important Direction
The Black Market survives by fusing a little bit of everything.
It is not pure Bio, pure Mecha, or pure Pure.
That mixed identity is part of why it endures.

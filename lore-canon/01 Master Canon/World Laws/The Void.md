# The Void

The Void is the prison-realm of the setting.

## Core Idea
Humanity in 3D is the diminished remainder or shadow of a higher-dimensional humanity that fell after war.

## Importance
The Void is not merely empty space.
It is a containment world tied to punishment, exile, limitation, and forced adaptation.

# The Seven Capital Sins

The seven capital sins are major metaphysical forces in the IP.

## Core Idea
The sins were originally placed as corruptive forces,
but over time evolution adapted them into pressures that also shape growth, struggle, and transformation.

## Important Direction
The sins do not only destroy.
They test.
They distort.
They reveal weakness.
They also force humanity to confront itself.

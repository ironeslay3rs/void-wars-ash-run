# Pantheon Structure

Pantheons in the IP are shattered remnants of older divine civilizations.

## Core Idea
The beings remembered as gods, demons, monsters, and myths are tied to the broken memory of higher wars, fallen powers, and conquered worlds.

## Important Direction
Pantheons should not feel like random mythology references.
Each pantheon must feel like a surviving cultural fracture of a greater divine history.

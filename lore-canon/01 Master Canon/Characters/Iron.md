# Iron

Iron is one of the central characters of the IP.

## Core Identity
He begins from exhaustion, struggle, and human limitation.
He is not introduced as a grand hero.
He becomes one through endurance, choice, suffering, and growth.

The audience should understand his pain before they understand his destiny.

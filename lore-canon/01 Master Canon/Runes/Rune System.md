# Rune System

Runes are one of the core power systems of the IP.

## Core Idea
Runes are crafted tools of power.
They do not define a person's level by themselves, but they amplify, store, focus, or express power.

## Core Laws
- Runes are tools, not absolute rank markers.
- Stronger individuals can usually hold more and better runes.
- Capacity matters.
- Compatibility matters.
- Level matters.
- Some runes can be stacked.
- Rune sets unlock extra effects when completed.

## Use This Note To Track
- rune materials
- rune crafting process
- body placement or containers

- rune set rules

- rune knight requirements

## Known Canon Anchors
- Level 1 rune sets are standard for war.
- Level 2 rune sets are Executional Rune Sets.
- Level 3 rune sets are rare.
- Level 4 rune sets are nearly impossible to acquire.
- Level 5 rune sets are mythical.
- Level 6 and above are beyond normal creation inside the Void.

# The Three Empires

The three empires rise from the larger conflict of the schools.

## The Three Empires
- Bio Empire
- Mecha Empire
- Pure Empire

## Core Idea
These empires are not just political bodies.
They are civilizational expressions of different evolutionary answers.

## Bio Empire
Strength, survival, flesh, adaptation, predation, and renewal.

## Mecha Empire
Intelligence, analysis, precision, structure, enhancement, and control.

## Pure Empire
Wisdom, memory, soul, inheritance, and spiritual law.

## Deeper Direction
The war between them never fully ends,
because each empire believes it understands the correct future of humanity.

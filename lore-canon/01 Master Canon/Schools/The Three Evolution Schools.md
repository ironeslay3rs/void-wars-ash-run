# The Three Evolution Schools

The three great evolution paths are the backbone of the setting.

## The Schools
- Bio
- Mecha
- Pure

## Core Idea
Each school represents a different answer to survival, growth, and escape from limitation.

## Bio
Body-focused evolution.
Adaptation through flesh, DNA, hunting, mutation, and living power.

## Mecha
Mind/control-focused evolution.
Adaptation through precision, enhancement, cybernetics, analysis, and engineered perfection.

## Pure
Soul/memory-focused evolution.
Adaptation through memory, spirit, fire, wisdom, and inner law.

## Higher Truth
The incomplete paths compete against one another for most of the saga.
The deeper truth is that body, mind, and soul must ultimately be fused.

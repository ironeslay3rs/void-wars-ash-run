# Humanity Theme

Humanity is one of the deepest themes in the IP.

## Core Idea
People become stronger not only through personal effort,
but through the sacrifices made for them by others.

## Beauty of Humanity
Humanity is beautiful because it endures, learns, adapts, remembers, forgives, suffers, and still keeps going.

## Key Theme Elements
- sacrifice
- family
- struggle
- memory
- adaptation
- evolution
- inherited strength
- pain transformed into meaning

## Important Direction
Humanity should never be treated as weak merely because it is incomplete.
Its incompleteness is part of its power and future becoming.

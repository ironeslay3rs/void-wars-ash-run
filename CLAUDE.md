## Project

Ash Run — 2D rhythm-metroidvania (Megaman + Mario + Hi-Fi Rush + Silksong + Hollow Knight fusion)

### Game DNA:

* Hollow Knight / Silksong — tight movement, interconnected map, atmosphere
* Hi-Fi Rush — rhythm-synced world, Perfect/Good/OK/Off timing grades
* Celeste — assist mode (no content locks), per-zone D→S ranks, proud death counter
* Metroid — 7-ability unlock sequence, gates open paths in ALL prior zones
* Dead Cells — distinct zone biomes with unique BPM, hazards, and palette
* Ori — emotional story via Memory Fragments (ghost echoes, letters, drawings)
* Shovel Knight — optional Echo/Fracture/Void challenge tiers + feats
* Megaman + Mario — precision controls + accessible difficulty curve
Studio: IronE Slay3rs
Universe: Sevenfold Rune

## Game Identity

Ash is an 8-year-old girl abducted by a Black City laboratory.
She has a rare Pure school ability: Resonance Sight — she can see mana
traces left by people who passed through locations before her.
These traces act as guides, warnings, and puzzle clues.

Her abilities come from her hybrid heritage:

* Mother: Pure school — gifted Ash with resonance sight from early age
* Father: Bio + Mecha hybrid — accelerated her brain and body development
This makes Ash physically capable beyond her age AND spiritually perceptive.

### World Structure — Interconnected Metroidvania:

The world is NOT linear levels. It is ONE interconnected map of zones joined
by ability-gated transitions. Ash returns to old areas with new abilities to
find secrets.

* Lab (starting zone) — tutorial; movement + basic Resonance Sight
* Black City outskirts — first real zone; rhythm mechanic introduced
* Empire territories — ability-gated, any order:
  - Verdant Coil (Bio empire)
  - Chrome Synod (Mecha empire)
  - Ember Vault (Pure empire)
* Deep Lab return — endgame; new abilities reveal old-zone secrets
* Reach Iron — story climax

Gates are opened by abilities (wall-jump, dash, on-beat Resonance Sight
reveal, future traversal tools). Order of empire visits is player choice.

### Core Mechanics:

* Resonance Sight: see past events as ghost trails — platforming guidance
* World Heartbeat: mana traces PULSE to a rhythm Ash feels through Pure heritage
  - On-beat jump = higher/further
  - On-beat dash = invincibility frames
  - On-beat Resonance Sight = reveals hidden paths
  - Off-beat = normal movement, no bonuses, no penalties (never punishing)
  - Bosses and hazard patterns sync to the beat — learn the rhythm to survive
* Zonal musical identity:
  - Lab: sterile, quiet, heartbeat only — rhythm emerges as Ash awakens
  - Verdant Coil: organic, tribal, heartbeat drums
  - Chrome Synod: electronic, precise, mechanical beats
  - Ember Vault: choral, resonant, soul-deep bass
  - Black City: chaotic, mixed, unpredictable tempo shifts
* Enhanced mobility: wall jumps, dashes, fast reflexes (Bio/Mecha heritage)
* No combat powers initially — survival through movement and observation
* Powers gradually awaken as she pushes her limits (evolution theme)
